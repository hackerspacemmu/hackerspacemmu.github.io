Who are we

Bongus! We're a group of hackers who build things and come together every Tuesday over at our man-cave somewhere at MMU to show off with each other. Hackers ? In contrast to the misconcepted belief that hackers are cyber-criminals or people worshipped by kids, we go by the actual definition of hacking whereby we create, build, and share.

Becoming a hacker is an investment

We're a group of people who are skilled in various different fields: designing, developing, websites, cyber-security, wood-work, hardware; you name it. Working with people such as these allows you to expand your own circle of skills.
